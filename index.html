Instructions:

Use the add and remove buttons for each of the various dice types to build your dice pool. As you add or remove dice the number inside each colored die will update to inform you of how many of each type of die you have added to your pool. When you have finished assembling your dice pool click the roll button to roll all of the dice in the pool. The total and net results of your dice rolls will be displayed in the text area.
